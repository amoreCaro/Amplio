Graduates, Apprenticeships & Placements  

Graduate Roles

  • Are you a recent graduate looking to start your career? Our graduate opportunities are designed to build on the knowledge gained in your degree and put theory into practice. You’ll be working on large, exciting projects, giving you an in-depth understanding of your chosen specialism, and an opportunity to learn about how the other departments & stakeholders of the business work together.

Apprenticeships

  • We offer apprenticeships for people studying part-time, training, or looking for a career change, providing you with crucial hands-on experience in trade.
  • We recognise the value in identifying and growing our future talent through apprenticeships which combine the theory with essential practical learning.
  • Whether it’s site or office based, you will gain first class on-the-job experience giving you the exposure and opportunity to learn and successfully complete your apprenticeship as well a range of technical training applicable to your discipline.

Placements

  • We work closely with universities to offer year-long & summer placements which provide hands on exposure to your chosen specialism and give a good introduction to the the technical, commercial, construction and project management teams.
  • You will get to know what life on-site is really like and be given the guidance and support you need to ensure you make the most of your time with us.
If these areas interest you please contact our careers team today:careers@micim.co.uk
